About

I'm an AI/ML Engineer and Technical Leader with experience building large-scale machine learning systems in fintech, adtech, and cloud infrastructure. I've worked at companies including Nubank and Google, leading teams that ship ML in production.

My work spans MLOps, real-time event AI, liveness detection, personalized video at scale, and data access control. I'm also passionate about ML careers and the engineering discipline behind reliable, maintainable ML systems.

Earlier in my career I did research in aspect-oriented programming, resulting in publications at SPLAT/ECOOP and the Journal of Object Technology, and a master's dissertation at USP.
